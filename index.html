Biography

Hi! I'm Xiao Chen (陈 骁), currently a first-year Ph.D. student at Multi-Media Lab (MMLab), The Chinese University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Tianfan Xue.

I've spent wonderful time as an on-site research intern at Shanghai AI Laboratory, working closely with Dr. Jiangmiao Pang. I received my B.Eng. Degree and Honors Degree (Advanced Class of Engineering Education, Chu Kochen Honors College) from Zhejiang University, China.

My current research interests lie in the intersection of 3D Vision and Robotics (Embodied AI). I'm open to potential collaborations, feel free to drop me an email if you are interested in :)
